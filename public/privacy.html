Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Data Collection

When you use phew., we collect the text you enter during your "brain dumps" and any associated metadata (like timestamps). This data is stored securely in your private Supabase database.

2. AI Processing

Your dump text is processed by the Google Gemini API to extract tasks and categorize items. Your data is sent to Gemini solely for processing and is not used for training their models without your explicit consent.

3. Local Storage

We use local storage ("Tester Mode") to persist your session and preferences on your device if you choose not to log in via a formal account.

4. Data Sovereignty

You can delete your tasks or your entire account at any time. When you delete data, it is removed from our active databases.
